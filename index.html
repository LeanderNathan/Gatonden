About Us

Our Vision

Ever felt like slurping those iconic ramen from "Naruto"? How about those steaks from "One Piece?" Maybe you want to try all those delecious gourmet from "Food wars". From Studio Ghibli's iconic delicacies to various mouth watering 2-d delicacies, Gatonden serves you the best dishes from anime brought to life.

Gatonden is the first anime themed food restaurant established in Bangalore. It exists for the sole reason of appeasing your appetite for those eye-catching, mouth-watering, iconic 2-D dishes brought to life. Situated at the heart of Bangalore, we became an instant hit with the locals and eventually, die hard anime fans from around the state and country started visiting us.

Today, Gatonden has taken a special place in all our customer's hearts and that drives our passion higher than ever to cook and serve you the best way possible!

So "Youkoso" to Gatonden, the authentic food palace.
